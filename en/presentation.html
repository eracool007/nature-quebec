Let’s Discover Web Development!

  • As part of the Web Development program offered at the Cégep de Saint-Félicien, we had a special mandate to accomplish for the Programmation des interfaces web class. In fact, we had to create a website presenting three touristic points of interest of our choice. However, these needed to respect the established guidelines. The goal was to put into practice the concepts acquired during our HTML, CSS and JavaScript courses.
  • The site needed a landing page and it had to be bilingual. In addition, points of interest had to be presented in the form of a carousel. Images, audio files and an HTML form had to be included as well(not actually sending information).
  • Having completed the mandate, I am now happy to present to you with this website, my project called Nature Québec. You will find three of my favorite parks in Quebec City, where I have the pleasure of going frequently to recharge my batteries. I hope you will have as much fun discovering them as I had doing this project!
